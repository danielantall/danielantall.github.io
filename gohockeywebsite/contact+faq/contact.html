Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups can participate in Go Hockey’s programs?

Go Hockey offers programs for girls of all ages, starting from beginner levels to advanced skill levels. Our programs cater to different age brackets, ensuring that every participant has a safe, fun, and supportive environment to learn and grow in hockey.

How do I register my child for a program?

You can register by visiting our website’s “Programs” page and selecting the program suited to your child’s age and skill level. Once you choose a program, simply follow the registration instructions provided.

What equipment is needed for participation?

Basic hockey gear is required, including a helmet, skates, gloves, and pads. We also offer resources to help families find affordable options for purchasing or renting equipment. For new players, we sometimes have loaner equipment available – feel free to ask!

How can I get updates on new programs or events?

Sign up for our newsletter on our website to receive the latest updates on new programs, events, and other exciting opportunities. You can also follow us on social media for real-time updates and community stories.

Are there any financial assistance options?

Yes, Go Hockey strives to make hockey accessible to all girls, regardless of financial situation. We offer financial assistance options and scholarships for qualifying families. Please reach out to our support team to discuss options.

Can I pay for programs in cash?

Yes, we do accept cash payments for program fees. To arrange a cash payment, please contact our office directly or visit us during our office hours. We’ll guide you through the process and provide a receipt for your records.

Is it possible to sign up for multiple programs at once?

Absolutely! We encourage participants to explore all that Go Hockey has to offer. During the registration process, you’ll have the option to enroll in multiple programs at once. Signing up for multiple sessions at the same time can also qualify for a discount. Please check our website or contact us for details on multi-program discounts.

How can I become a coach or volunteer with Go Hockey?

We’re always looking for passionate coaches and volunteers who want to make a difference. To join our team, please visit the “Get Involved” page on our website and complete the volunteer or coaching application. All coaches and volunteers must go through a background check and attend training sessions to ensure the best experience for our participants. Whether you're interested in coaching, assisting on the ice, or supporting events, we’d love to have you on board!
