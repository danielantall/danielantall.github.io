About Us

GO Hockey Leadership and Development is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering female hockey athletes to reach their full potential both on and off the ice. Our mission is to cultivate leadership skills, confidence, and resilience among young women, establishing a supportive community, and to increase the number of girls exposed to and continuing in hockey and sports. By offering inclusive programs for girls of all skill levels and income backgrounds, GO Hockey strives to eliminate barriers and ensure that every girl who wants to learn and play hockey has the chance to do so!
